MY MOMENTS WITH YOU

We’re on a run and I’m feeling in love with you.

October 27, 2022

We’re on a run and I feel weightless.

Well, actually I’m in a lot of pain now, but just a few minutes ago, I felt like I could run forever. For as long as we had things to talk about, I could keep putting one step in front of another. And how could we not? Our favorite thing to do together is literally to do nothing, or anything together.

Now though, the miles are catching up, and I need to concentrate to run. Still, I’m feeling in love with you.
