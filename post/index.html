You’ve fallen asleep on my arm and I’m feeling in love with you

You’ve fallen asleep on my arm and I’m feeling in love with you Sometimes, in the comfort of thoughts formed half asleep, and get lost in thoughts haves and wants. Me? I want your quiet love. The love that’s there, and steady. The love of you falling asleep, curled into my chest, your body relaxing into mine. Your love, having remembered to set an alarm for a call early tomorrow, and mine, timing my breathing to match your movements.

We’re running around in a stone forest and I’m in a better mood when you’re around

We’re running around in a stone forest and I’m in a better mood when you’re around. No seriously, I feel like half the time we’re just being, I have an ear to ear grin and I want to hug and squeeze you and go grocery shopping. But today, we’re dancing along to a Taekwondo song, galavanting through a hike, with the nature around us all etched stone. We’re racing around and exploring what we can see, being as loud as we are until we remember to be quiet.
