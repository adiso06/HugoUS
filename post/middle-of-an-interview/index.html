It’s the middle of an interview, and I keep messaging you.

It’s nice, you know, to be able to share stuff. An Instagram reel of a cat overreaching, or bizarre things like a program requiring us to work ungodly hours. I want to immediately share with you. Or even nonsense because I want to see a notification saying ‘qt’ has messaged me back. It’s comfortable, like a being wrapped in a warm blanket while watching a movie at night, or listening to soft music as we drive into nothing. In between messaging you these small random thoughts, I’m feeling in love with you.
